Features

Cozy Cottages

Our guests stay in comfortable, modern cottages overlooking the sea. Each cottage is within easy walking distance to our taco trucks, ice cream stands, bars, restaurants, and petting zoos.

Adorable Cats

Each of our cottages comes fully stocked with all the amenties your feline companions will need. Let us choose a variety of cats for your stay, or make a request!
